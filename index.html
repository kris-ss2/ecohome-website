Making Old Buildings More Sustainable

EcoHome Solutions is a small startup idea focused on helping old buildings reduce energy waste and become more environmentally friendly.

Why This Project?

Many old buildings use too much electricity and create unnecessary carbon emissions. Our idea is to improve insulation, lighting systems and renewable energy usage in affordable ways.

Main Goals

  • Reduce energy waste
  • Support sustainable living
  • Make renovations more affordable
